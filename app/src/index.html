Hospital Management System



















About

Hospital Management System is a webapp for solving the day to day problem faced by the hospital employees and the patients. This will help the patient to consult with the doctor even when they are at there home. They can talk to doctors on online platform through chat pannel

Hospital Management System also delivers a paper free report system as using this hospital can now directly upload the report on user console so that user have not to come to hospital to get there reports.
